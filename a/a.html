 Wilhelm Richard Wagner 

Wilhelm Richard Wagner is wanted for contibuting in the attempted uprising in Dresden earlier this this year.

The uprising took place from May 3rd - 9th, Wagner was last seen on the 11th in Dresden

Wilhelm Richard Wagner has been charged with:

  • assisting in political conflict
  • Failing to appear in court
  • utter forged documents

Wilhelm Richard Wagner was born on 22 May 1813 in Leipzig.

He is a male, 5' 7" tall, white, he is currently 36.

Wilhelm Richard Wagner should be considered dangerous. If you see Wilhelm Richard Wagner and are not ready for danger, do not approach or communicate with him. Contact us or Crime Stoppers if you have any information.

If you are prepared for danger however, there is a bounty of 1500 thaler for his capture.

If you see any of these people, do not go near them or talk to them. Contact us or Crime Stoppers if you have information about these people.
